How Project P started

Kevin Van Gastel

Project P, started as a little project to keep track of my work and to see how much I would grow over time.

Making and Instagram page for my new car and posting pictures is where it started for me. I wanted to improve them, so I started using Lightroom to give them that little extra.  The more I do it the better my pictures get and the more different options I learn. 

Sometimes I need to use Photoshop to change something in the picture. This is something I learned in school during my course for web design. 

Videos I have been editing as soon as I had phone with a camera. It started with filming my self gaming and making montages of it. 

Later on I started making videos of my dogs and started making little montages of it. I really enjoyed doing this.
